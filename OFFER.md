# OFFER.md
*Phase 1 Grand-Slam Offer for Ramsey AI — Solo Roofers Edition*

---

## 1. Business Snapshot
- **Business:** Ramsey AI — productized lead-generation agency for non-technical local service businesses
- **Selling:** A done-for-you system that turns Meta ads into booked roof-inspection appointments on the owner's calendar, with a 45-second response time
- **Current stage:** Phase 1 of the $100k/month blueprint — building 3 case studies before charging
- **Main channel:** Outbound DM + Loom audits → free 30-day pilots → paid retainers

---

## 2. Target Market
- **Market:** Local residential service trades
- **Segment:** Solo / 1–10 employee roofing contractors, $300k–$1.5M annual revenue, in secondary storm-prone metros (Tulsa, OKC, Wichita, Springfield MO, Greensboro NC)
- **Why this segment:** They feel acute lead-flow pain, already spend $500–$5k/mo on Angi/HomeAdvisor (validated buyers), have $5k–$25k ticket sizes that make ROI math undeniable, and live outside the saturation zone of Tier-1 metro agencies

---

## 3. Ideal Customer Avatar
**One-sentence avatar:** *Mike, 42, owner-operator of a 4-person roofing crew in Tulsa doing ~$700k/year, who left a regional roofer 3 years ago to start his own thing, knows the trade cold, but hates marketing and feels his business is one slow month away from a panic move.*

- **Current situation:**
  - Runs Angi/HomeAdvisor at ~$1,200/month, closes maybe 2% of leads
  - Door-knocks after storms, hates it, can't scale past himself
  - Has a Wix site he hasn't touched in 18 months
  - Tracks jobs in JobNimbus or a spreadsheet
- **Pain points (their words):**
  - *"My phone hasn't rung in two weeks."*
  - *"Every storm chaser from Texas shows up and steals my jobs."*
  - *"I'm a roofer, not a marketer."*
  - *"I need two full retails this month or I'm laying Miguel off."*
- **Failed attempts:**
  - Angi / HomeAdvisor / CraftJack (shared, low-intent, expensive)
  - Hired a "digital marketing guy" who built a website and disappeared
  - Tried running his own FB ads and burned $800 with no calls
  - Asked his nephew to "do something on Instagram"
- **Desired outcome:** A predictable flow of homeowners asking *him specifically* to come look at their roof, without him having to hustle, door-knock, or hope a storm hits

---

## 4. Dream Outcome
- **Primary outcome:** *10+ qualified roof inspections booked on the calendar each month, on autopilot, exclusively for his business*
- **Emotional outcome:** Sleep through the night. Stop waking up at 5am worried about payroll. Show his wife the calendar full of bookings.
- **Status shift:** From "scrappy roofer chasing leads" to "the booked-out roofer in town"
- **Outcome statement (use verbatim in marketing):**
  > **"Wake up every Monday with a full week of roof inspections already on your calendar — no door-knocking, no Angi, no chasing."**

---

## 5. Obstacles (the things blocking the dream outcome)
1. They don't have time or skill to write/test ad copy
2. Their website doesn't convert visitors into calls
3. Leads come in but go unanswered for hours → cold by the time owner calls back
4. Owner is on a roof during business hours, can't pick up the phone
5. Leads don't know if it's a real local roofer or a national lead-broker
6. Calls back from "888" numbers go to voicemail forever
7. No way to qualify leads — burns time on tire-kickers
8. Calendar/scheduling is manual phone tag
9. After-hours leads (evenings, weekends) get zero response
10. Storm hits → can't react fast enough to capture demand
11. No tracking — owner doesn't know which ads or sources actually work
12. Tried FB ads alone, didn't know how to target or optimize
13. Got burned by a "marketing agency" once, deeply skeptical of all of them
14. Cash-flow tight — can't pay $2k upfront before seeing results
15. Hates technology, won't use complex software

## 7. Core Offer

### Offer Name (Primary)
**The Booked Roof Engine™**
*"We make your phone ring with booked roof inspections — in under 45 seconds — without door-knocking or paying Angi."*

### Alternate names to A/B test in outreach
- The 45-Second Roof Lead System
- The Roofer's Phone-Ringer

### What's included (full deliverable)

8. **Weekly Plain-English Report** — "this week you got X inspections booked, Y showed up, Z became jobs"
9. **Monthly Strategy Call** — 30 minutes with you, no jargon, just numbers and what to change

### Format
Fully done-for-you. Owner action required = **read texts + show up to inspections.**

### Delivery
- Day 1–3: Setup (landing page, ad accounts, SMS, calendar integration)
- Day 4: Ads launch
- Day 5+: First booked inspections start arriving

### Time to first win
**First booked inspection: 5–10 days from kickoff.**

## 10. Guarantee

### Pilot Guarantee (first 3 clients only)
> **"You pay $0 to me for 30 days. You only cover $500 of Meta ad spend. If we don't deliver at least 10 qualified roof inspection requests in 30 days, you owe me nothing — and you keep the landing page and SMS system at no cost. All I ask in return is a 60-second video testimonial if it works."**

### Post-Pilot Guarantees — 3 options

**Option A — Unconditional money-back (low risk to client, high risk to you)**
> "30-day money back on Month 1. No questions asked."

**Option B — Performance guarantee (recommended)**
> **"If we don't deliver at least [X] booked inspections in your first 60 days, we work for free until we do. You'll never pay a retainer for a month without performance."**
*X is set per metro based on pilot data — typically 8–15 inspections.*

**Option C — Cost-recovery guarantee (most aggressive, best for sales)**
> "If our system doesn't generate enough closed-job revenue in 90 days to pay for itself 3x over, we refund every dollar you paid us."

### Recommended Guarantee
**Option B (Performance Guarantee)** for post-pilot. It's the right balance:
- Strong enough to crush objections from a burned-once roofer
- Tied to your actual deliverable (booked inspections) — not to *their* closing skill, which you can't control
- Won't bankrupt you if a client's market is slower than expected

Sell with this line:
> *"I only get paid when your phone is ringing. If it stops ringing, I work for free until it starts again."*
