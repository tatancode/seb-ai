## Copy Tone Rules (for whoever implements + future edits)

**Always use:**
- "Booked inspections" (not "leads")
- "45 seconds" (specific number, not "fast")
- "Exclusive to one roofer per metro" (the differentiator, repeated)
- "Your name on the lawn signs" / "your phone ringing" (visual, concrete)
- Direct address: "you," "your"
- Short sentences. Short paragraphs.

**Never use:**
- "AI" or "AI-powered"
- "Workflow," "pipeline," "automation"
- "Leverage," "synergy," "transform"
- "Scale your business" / "next-level"
- Anything that sounds like a SaaS landing page

**Test for every line:**
Would Mike-the-Tulsa-roofer-on-his-truck-during-lunch say *"yeah, that's me"* or *"what the hell is this guy talking about"*? If the second — rewrite.
